Director: Professor, Nancy Takeshta. I am American, born in the city of San Diego, California. I am a professor of English for international students. Objectives

Objectives of Belgrano English School:

  • To immerse yourself in the language, perfect or maintain it by practicing with a native English teacher.
  • Perfecting pronunciation.
  • Conversation, grammar, specific vocabulary (daily life, music, traveling, business, cooking, etc.).
  • Reading (newspapers, articles).
  • Workshops for writing and translation.
